UP Elections 2017

Narendra Modi is running through sadhe saati, so the upcoming days are going to be very challenging for him. While it is not at all likely that he should lose these set of UP elections, the opposition shall try with all its might to defeat him and there shall be plenty of challenges during that time.

BJP hasn't announced the CM candidate but they are banking on Modi because he's running through favorable mahadasha and antardasha and Sabka Saath Sabka Vikas like slogans has given an alternative for public because UP is mired with rampant corruption and hooliganism.
