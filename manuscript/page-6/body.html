Political future

Modi is among the few successful global influencers from India. The mahadasha of Moon in his Ascendant lord has led him to achieve much and reach great heights. This mahadasha of Moon lasts until about year 2020 after which a new phase in life or an altogether new adventure is indicated with an auspicious mahadasha of Mars.

Mars holds more promise in politics despite the antardashas of Rahu & Ketu that may bring challenges and difficulties for him in future. He will succeed with courage and positivity but may not be able to hold his position of power on his own terms. His well marketed initiatives such as the Jan Dhan Yojana, Make in India and Digital India will be the cornerstone of his vision for India. The world will remember Modi for his futuristic outlook and focus on development.
