Spirituality and Politics

The presence of Ketu in his eleventh house reiterates the presence of an invisible hand of the divine in materializing his victory. It also makes him appear to be of a spiritual & altruistic disposition to at least those who approve of him and follow him.

However, since Rahu sits in his 5th House of Higher Learning, his quest for self-actualization was halted half way and he had to rather walk the troubled route of Indian Politics in order to accomplish something in the more material and tangible world.

His Rahu aspects his 9th house of dharma and the 1st house of self. This makes him a shrewd politician who does not discriminate between good and evil actions and is able to take a tough stand to accomplish his goals.
